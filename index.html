Non-Custodial Architecture

How does non-custodial trading work?

You maintain complete control of your private keys and funds. Our system provides trading signals and analysis, but all transactions are executed through your own wallet with your explicit approval. We never store or have access to your private keys.

Is my wallet secure?

Yes, we never store or have access to your private keys. Your wallet connection is established through secure protocols, and you can disconnect at any time. All transactions require your explicit approval.

Tokenomics & Rewards

Why are rewards now paid in $MSKCAT instead of $SOL?

This change enhances the utility and value of $MSKCAT tokens while creating a more sustainable ecosystem. Token holders benefit from increased demand and utility, creating a positive feedback loop for the entire community.

Difference between Holder Rewards and the Referral Program?

These are two separate and independent earning opportunities:

Holder Rewards are passive earnings you receive automatically for holding 200,000 or more $MSKCAT tokens in your Telegram wallet. You earn a proportional share of the platform's trading fees through our buyback and redistribution mechanism. No action is required beyond holding your tokens.

The Referral Program allows you to actively earn commissions by inviting new users to the platform. You earn a percentage of the trading fees generated by the users you refer, across four tiers. The more your network grows and trades, the more you earn.

You can participate in one or both programs simultaneously. They do not overlap or conflict with each other.

How are my holdings verified?

We use automated verification through the Helius API to check $MSKCAT holdings in connected wallets, ensuring fair and accurate reward distribution. This process is transparent and happens automatically.
